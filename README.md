### Lab 2.

##### Pinout

Display (GMG12864-06D Ver. 2.2):

- K - GND
- A - 3.3V + 10kΩ
- VSS - GND
- VDD - 3.3V
- SCL - 13
- SI - 11
- CS - 10
- RS - 9
- RSE - 8

RTC (DS3231):

- \- - GND
- \+ - 5V
- SDA - 20
- SCL - 21

Humidity and temperature sensor (DHT11):

- \- - GND
- \+ - 5V
- SDA - 4

Photoresitor:

- 1st leg - 5V
- 2nd leg - A0, 10kΩ + GND
